Stress reliever

Origami can help reduce stress by encouraging focus and calmness during the folding process. As a person concentrates on each step, it can aid clear the mind and promote relaxation, making origami a peaceful and enjoyable activity.

Expression of Creativity

Origami allows people to express their creativity by turning simple paper into different designs and figures. By choosing how to fold and shape the paper, individuals can explore new ideas and create something unique using their imagination.

Improves Focus and Patience

Origami improves hand control and coordination. Folding carefully helps your fingers work better for writing, drawing, and other activities.
